# SocialHub

A mobile-first landing page that keeps all of our community links in a single, beautiful location.

## Features

- Full-screen scroll snapping on mobile for a jump-to-section browsing experience
- Rich social cards for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube, and TikTok
- Highlight section describing what visitors can expect from the community
- Newsletter signup form with responsive layout and focus states

## Getting started

1. Open `index.html` in your favorite browser.
2. Customize the social handles and URLs in the links section.
3. Optionally adjust the colors and branding inside `styles.css` to match your identity.
